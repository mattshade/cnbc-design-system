Case study

AI Enablement · NBC News Group

Executive-ready visibility and delivery for Data AI & Emerging Tech: a weekly rhythm of progress, governance, and hands-on enablement across newsroom, product, and engineering.

Strategy Custom agents Training Platforms

Operating model

  • Weekly cadence: A consistent report format for leadership—what moved, what blocked, and what was next across agents, training, and platform initiatives.
  • OKRs & prioritization: Helped define outcome-driven OKRs for AI enablement and capabilities, connecting tool adoption and applied research to measurable newsroom and enterprise use cases.
  • Execution hygiene: Drove translation of AI work into structured, scoped Jira stories—improving visibility, ownership, and prioritization across teams.

Portfolio highlights

Custom Copilot & agent programs. Built and advanced domain-specific agents across editorial, standards, legal, audience, and production workflows—with clear ownership, usage guidance, and practical rollout support.

Standards & policy assistant

Helped teams quickly reference newsroom standards, style guidance, and policy language in-context while drafting and reviewing content.

Legal & rights intake assistant

Structured first-pass legal support by routing common rights/questions to the right docs and teams, reducing ad hoc back-and-forth.

Editorial discovery copilot

Supported pitch and research workflows by surfacing relevant source context, angle starters, and follow-up questions for producers and reporters.

Booking & scheduling workflow assistant

Improved production coordination by standardizing recurring booking/scheduling steps and reducing operational friction for teams.

AI tools office hours

What started as ChatGPT office hours evolved into a broader AI tools office hours format covering creation, coding, meeting productivity, voice, and workflow automation. Sessions focused on live use cases and immediate application, not generic demos.

Training & enablement. Office hours were paired with GitHub Copilot sessions with Microsoft, Google Cloud AI topics (Gemini, Vertex, agent patterns), and cohort-style learning so teams could bring real tasks and leave with repeatable workflows.

Platform & data foundations. Progress toward a unified Data Hub mental model—helping teams discover and evaluate datasets with clearer documentation and tiers—alongside pragmatic license and tooling coordination across the generative AI stack.
